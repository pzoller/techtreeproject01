Background

When I was a teenager my parent's bought me the Avalon Hill game Panzer Leader. That was my first exposure to wargames. I did buy a few Avalon Hill and SPI gmees as a teenager. Though I was not able to find many wargame players when I was young. I did run into a few D&D players, back when the white box version was available.

I started player with other people when a local gamestore openned its backroom as an open playing area. From there I met a group of other young gentlemen that wound up being friends that I have known for decades. We had played lots of different tabletop and miniature war games.

I have tappered off playing table top role playing games due to having a busy schedule. Family and work allows me to do more things than play RPGs. I am still interested in gaming, and my current favorite game system is GURPS, published by Steve Jackson Gems.
